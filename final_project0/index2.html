中英文摘要

作者： Yang

水资源短缺的问题已经足够可怕，如果这仅有的水资源还不干净，我们又怎能继续生存？目前，水污染对中国社会的影响主要体现在经济、民生、社会稳定和生态领域，却绝不局限于此，中国在防治水污染、保护水资源上还大有可为。在看过这些触目惊心的数字和图片之后，你是愿意一起来改变世界，还是继续在冷漠无知中沉沦？

Water shortage problem has become so severe that it makes us wonder how human being can survive in the future if we cannot clean the precious water we have now? The influence of water pollution on the Chinese society reflects on the economy, people’s livelihood, social stability, ecology and more. Therefore, there is still a long way for us to go in preventing water pollution and protecting water resources. After reading these shocking statistics and pictures, are you going to join us and make some efforts to change our society or just stay ignorant and cold as you might always do?
